Jhih-Sian Lin


I am a graduate student at the Institute of Electrical and Control Engineering, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU), Taiwan. My research interests focus on Remote Vital Sign Sensing and Signal and Image Processing.

Bio: Jhih-Sian Lin received the B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Chung Yuan Christian University (CYCU), Taiwan. She is currently pursuing her master degree at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU).
