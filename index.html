Jhih-Sian Lin


I am a master degree student at the Chaotic Systems and Signal Processing Laboratory (CSSP Lab.) at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU), Taiwan. My research interests focus on Remote Vital Sign Sensing and Signal and Image Processing.

Bio: Jhih-Sian Lin received the B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Chung Yuan Christian University (CYCU), Taiwan. She is currently pursuing her master degree at the Institute of Electrical and Control Engineering, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU), where she is advised by Prof. Wu. Her current research interests include remote photoplethysmography (rPPG), signal processing, image processing, and control systems.
